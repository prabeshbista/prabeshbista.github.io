My Story

Hi! It's me Prabesh Bista hailing from Nepal. I was born and grew up in Kathmandu. I did my undergraduate at Tri-Chandra Multiple Campus. After completing my undergraduate, I came to Taiwan for internship. Then I worked as a Research Assistant for a year at Dr. Cheng-Tien Chiang's lab . I am a PhD student at his lab. I am studying the electron interaction in solid using laser-based photoelectron spectroscopy. My current works involve designing and constructing an experiment and analyzing the data. Besides doing experiments I will be doing these activities like reading newspaper and non-fiction books, hiking, playing football, cycling, traveling, watching documenteries and movies, and cooking food.
