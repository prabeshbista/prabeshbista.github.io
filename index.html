My Story

I hail from Nepal. I was born and grew up in Kathmandu. I did my undergraduate at Tri-Chandra Multiple Campus. After completing my undergraduate, I came to Taiwan for internship. Then I worked as a Research Assistant for a year at Dr. Cheng-Tien Chiang's lab . I am a PhD student at his lab. I am studying the electron interaction in solid using laser-based photoelectron spectroscopy.

I have a different kinds of hobbies. I like reading newspaper, hiking, playing football, cycling, traveling, and cooking food. While staying abroad I learned to cook food.
