Project information

NOPA stands for Non-collinear optical parametric amplifier. It is driven by pulse of 20 micro joule, 1030 nm , and repetition rate up to 2 MHz. It is made up from different optical components. There are different non linear phenomenas like second harmonic generation, third harmonic generation, white light generation and optical parametric amplication . The highly intensed, short pulse duration, and tunable wavelengths are generated from this set up. This light source is useful for photoemission spectroscopy. These visible wavelengths are absorbed by most of the materials which will unravel the electronic structure. The schematic diagram of Nopa is adapted from the thesis of Emanuel Wittmann.
